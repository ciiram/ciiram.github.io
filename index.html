Brief Bio

I am an Associate Professor at Dedan Kimathi University of Technology in Nyeri, Kenya where I teach electrical engineering and also conduct research in a number of areas including bioacoustics, IoT, machine learning and data science. Since September 2019 I have led the Centre for Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (DSAIL). I also serve as the board chair of Data Science Africa.

Prior to joining DeKUT in 2013, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Sheffield between 2011 and 2013, a PhD student at Drexel University in Philadelphia, USA between 2007 and 2011 and a BSc Student at the University of Nairobi between 2002 and 2007.
